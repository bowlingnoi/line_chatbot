## Shipping Rates and Pricing

**Q: What are the shipping rates for Bangkok?**
A: For standard delivery within Bangkok, our rates start at 50 THB for packages up to 2kg. Rates increase based on weight and distance.

**Q: How much does it cost to ship to other provinces?**
A: Shipping to other provinces (upcountry) starts at 80 THB for packages up to 2kg. Express delivery options are available with higher rates for faster service.
- For Flash express starts at 18 THB
- For Shopee starts at 20 THB
- For Thailand Post starts at 25 THB
- For KEX starts at 27 THB

**Q: Are there any additional fees?**
A: Additional fees may apply for:
- Cash on Delivery (COD) service: 25 THB
- Oversized packages (above 100x100x100 cm)
- Remote area delivery
- Insurance coverage (optional)

**Q: Do you offer bulk shipping discounts?**
A: Yes! Businesses shipping more than 100 packages per month are eligible for volume discounts. Please contact our sales team at sales@shippop.com for custom pricing.

## Delivery Time

**Q: How long does standard delivery take?**
A: Standard delivery times:
- Bangkok: 1-2 business days
- Central region: 2-3 business days
- Other provinces: 3-5 business days
- Remote areas: 4-7 business days

**Q: Do you offer same-day delivery?**
A: Yes, same-day delivery is available in Bangkok for orders placed before 12:00 PM. The service fee is 150 THB.

**Q: What about express delivery?**
A: Express delivery reduces delivery time by 1-2 days and is available for most destinations at an additional cost of 50 THB.

## Order Tracking

**Q: How can I track my package?**
A: You can track your package in several ways:
1. Use the tracking number sent to your email or SMS
2. Visit our website at shippop.com/tracking
3. Use the Shippop mobile app
4. Contact our customer service at 02-xxx-xxxx

**Q: I haven't received my tracking number. What should I do?**
A: Tracking numbers are sent within 2 hours of package pickup. If you haven't received it:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Log into your Shippop account to view the tracking number
3. Contact our support team with your order number

**Q: My tracking shows no updates. Is my package lost?**
A: Not necessarily. Tracking may not update immediately for several reasons:
- Package is in transit between facilities
- Delay in scanning at sorting centers
- Weekend or holiday delays
If there's no update for more than 24 hours, please contact our support team.

## Returns and Refunds

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: Our return policy varies by merchant. Generally:
- Items can be returned within 7-14 days
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Return shipping fees may apply
Contact the seller directly for specific return terms.

**Q: How do I get a refund for a lost package?**
A: If your package is confirmed lost:
1. File a claim within 30 days of shipment
2. Provide proof of shipment and value
3. Our team will investigate (7-14 business days)
4. If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

**Q: Can I get a refund for late delivery?**
A: Yes, if delivery is delayed beyond our guaranteed delivery time:
- Contact customer service within 48 hours of expected delivery
- We'll verify the delay reason
- Eligible cases receive shipping fee refund or credit
